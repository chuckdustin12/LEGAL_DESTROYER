Court Coordinator's duties include assisting the administrative judges. See Tex. Gov't Code Ann. 
§ 74.102(b) which provides as follows:

coordinators shall cooperate with regional presiding and local administrative 
judges and state agencies having duties in the area of the operation of the courts.

administrative judges. Only the judges, not the litigants, have the authority to direct the activities 
of the court coordinators and clerks. Mr. Myers' objections regarding the involvement are 
overruled.
