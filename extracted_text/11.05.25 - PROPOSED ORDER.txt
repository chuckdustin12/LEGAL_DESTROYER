On this day, the Court considered Respondent Charles Dustin Myers' Motion for No-
Evidence Summary Judgment filed pursuant to Texas Rule of Civil Procedure 166a(i). After
examining the motion, any response filed by Petitioner Morgan Michelle Myers, the pleadings,
and other evidence properly before the Court, the Court finds as follows:

1. Respondent's Motion for No-Evidence Summary Judgment was properly filed after an

II. No evidence of the existence of an active protective order with a finding of family 
violence; 
III. No evidence supporting Petitioner's claim of financial indigence when filing suit; 
IV. No evidence of proper notice and hearing regarding the February 1, 2024 
Temporary Orders; 
V. No evidence of Respondent's agreement to the March 14, 2024 "Agreed" Orders;

VI. No evidence that the current Temporary Orders are in the best interests of the

3. Petitioner has failed to produce more than a scintilla of evidence raising a genuine issue

4. Petitioner's deemed admissions resulting from failure to respond to discovery further

IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED, ADJUDGED, AND DECREED THAT: 
i. Respondent Charles Dustin Myers' Motion for No-Evidence Summary Judgment is 
hereby GRANTED in all respects. 
ii. The Court finds that Petitioner failed to substantiate her claim of financial indigence filed 
on December 18, 2023, and this matter may be subject to dismissal under Texas Practice 
and Remedies Code § 13.001. 
iii. The Temporary Orders dated March 14, 2024, are hereby VACATED, as there is no 
evidence supporting their entry or continuation. 
iv. All relief not expressly granted herein is DENIED.
